Geraldine Thomas is a graduate of Dalhousie University in Halifax with a Ph.D. in Classics. She has been on the faculty of Saint Mary’s University in Halifax for over 30 years. She is an Associate Professor of Classics, teaching courses such as Greek and Roman History, History of Ancient Art, Women in Angiquity and Latin. She has traveled to Greece many times to see the ancient sites, enjoy the food and culture, and to enjoy the sheer verve of life in Greece. She has numerous among the Greek community in Halifax, and has published several articles on the Greek communities in the Maritimes.
